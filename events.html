Event 1: Getaway to Yesterday

17 DEC, 8.30 pm -10.30pm, MPH

The Korean Language Society and RentakMMU dance club hosted a joint bonding event on December 17th from 8:30 PM to 10:30 PM in MPH. The event was a success, with members from both clubs enjoying the opportunity to connect and learn from each other. The event featured a variety of activities, including a dance performance by RentakMMU, a Korean language lesson, and a potluck dinner. The event was a great way for members of both clubs to bond and build relationships.

Event 2: K-ONZ!

20 Nov - 22 Nov, 8.30 am - 5.00pm, Rimbun Ilmu

The Korean Cultural Festival, a three-day celebration from December 20th to 22nd, offered a captivating immersion into Korean traditions and arts. The festival featured a diverse range of activities, including captivating performances of traditional Korean dance, music, and martial arts. Attendees could also participate in engaging workshops on Hanbok wearing, calligraphy, traditional crafts, and Korean language. A vibrant atmosphere was further enhanced by delicious Korean food stalls and a lively K-Pop dance competition. Screenings of popular Korean films and dramas provided insights into contemporary Korean culture and entertainment.

Event 3: Bonding Night

10 Nov, 8.30 pm -10.30pm, FCM Atrium

Korean Language Society hosted a fun and memorable bonding night at FCM Atrium, filled with activities to strengthen connections among members. The event featured icebreakers, team-based trivia, and interactive stations like group charades, a "build-it-together" challenge, and karaoke. A heartfelt sharing circle allowed members to reflect on their experiences within the club, fostering deeper connections. The night ended with a cozy meal, laughter, and a themed photo booth for keepsakes. The event successfully brought members closer together, energizing everyone for future initiatives.
